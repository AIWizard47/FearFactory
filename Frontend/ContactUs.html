Frequently Asked Questions

How scary are your experiences?

Our experiences range from mildly spooky to extremely terrifying. Each experience has a fear rating to help you choose the right level of horror for your tolerance.

Is there an age requirement?

Most of our experiences require participants to be at least 16 years old. Some of our more intense experiences have an 18+ requirement. Please check the specific experience details.

Can I get a refund if I'm too scared?

We do not offer refunds for being scared - that's what you came for! However, if you need to leave early, our staff will safely escort you out of the experience.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 6 or more. Contact our booking department for more information on group packages and corporate events.
