FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a custom project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple projects may take as little as 2 weeks, while more complex projects can take 8 weeks or more. During our consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What information do you need to get started?

+

The more information you can provide, the better. At minimum, we need to know your project goals, target audience, desired features, and any specific horror elements you want to include. References to existing projects you like are also helpful. Our project request form will guide you through providing all necessary information.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

+

Yes, we offer various support packages to ensure your project continues to run smoothly after launch. These range from basic technical support to comprehensive maintenance plans. We'll discuss support options during the proposal phase.

What is your payment structure?

+

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we may establish a milestone-based payment schedule. All payment terms will be clearly outlined in our proposal.

Can you work with existing designs or code?

+

Yes, we can work with existing designs, code, or content. Whether you need to enhance an existing project or completely overhaul it with a horror theme, we can accommodate your needs. Please provide details about your existing assets in the project request form.
